THE OVERVIEW

Hosanna Praise Bulb International (HP BULB) is a non denominational fellowship with a mandate to restore righteousness via the power of praise psalm 67:3-7 HP BULB Serves as a resouce center for the church in the training of belivers in the art of intercession , praise and worship , strategist spiritual warfare and discipleship.
To accomplish this task , HP BULB accepts invitations to organize and conduct praise programmes, music carnival, Crusades, conferences, seminars, youth convention. HP BULB recognize and upholds the world view of the Holy Bible conduct of family life, government, business and the promotion of freedom and true democracy.
We affrim the centrality of praise and prayers in establishing the kingdom of GOD in the earth

THE OBEJETIVES

Praising and interceding and to seek GOD for the establishment of His kingdom in the nation earth
To establish a house fellowship in the cities of all the earth
To enforce the finished work of christ through the power of praise for the benefits of indiviuals,families institution and communities.
To raise and train responsible servants leaders for public life.
Transform the royal traditional institution into godly custodians of the law and culture.
To promote and moble the kingdom lifestyle in the family as work place.
To establish disciple into the upcoming generation to take responsibility for the nation Nigeria.
To partner with the loacl churches and seek their active participation in all the activities of the ministry.

CORPORATE VISION

Establishing of kingdom in the natures of the earth,people and institution
