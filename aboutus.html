THE OVERVIEW

Hosanna Praise Bulb International (HP BULB) is a non denominational fellowship with a mandate to restore righteousness via the power of praise psalm 67:3-7

HP BULB Serves as a resouce center for the church in the training of belivers in the art of intercession , praise and worship , strategist spiritual warfare and discipleship.
To accomplish this task , HP BULB accepts invitations to organize and conduct praise programmes, music carnival, Crusades, conferences, seminars, youth convention.

HP BULB recognize and upholds the world view of the Holy Bible conduct of family life, government, business and the promotion of freedom and true democracy.
We affrim the centrality of praise and prayers in establishing the kingdom of GOD in the earth
